Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Welcome to Femina. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identifiers like your name or email. Any data you enter (cycle dates, symptoms, moods) is stored locally on your device.

Sensitive Health Data: Information regarding your menstrual cycle, symptoms, and health notes are stored locally in the app's database. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly export or share it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate cycle predictions and health insights.
  • To personalize your app experience (e.g., Muslim Mode settings).
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your health data is stored using Android's Room Database on your local storage. We recommend using the built-in Biometric Lock feature in the app settings to prevent unauthorized access to your device.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@Femina.com
